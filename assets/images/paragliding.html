Sikkim Adventure Tour Packages

Sikkim is a land full of gusto where you not only get to sight mesmerising peaks but also trek and mount them. As a matter of fact, Sikkim is one of the popular places in India for adventure fanatics. The state feels millions of thumps on its ground every year as the adrenaline junkies march to its hills, alpine lakes, emerald meadows, gushing rivers, and even the snow clad mountains. In this mountainous state, our travel package for the adventurous souls is like the safest hide as we pack them with several adventure activities, safe camping experience, best transportation arrangements and on-time departures and receptions.

Our adventure expert team meticulously plans aventure trekking holidays to breathtaking trails like Goecha La, Kanchenjunga, Dzongri, Green Lake along with mountaineering expeditions to some of the best peaks in Sikkim like Jopuno, Frey’s Peak, Tinchenkhang, Byrmkhangse and Lamo Angden. Our Sikkim adventure tour packages also include offers other thrilling sports like rafting in the mighty rivers of Teesta and Rangeet; paragliding as well as hang gliding and mountain biking. With our travel experts, you need not worry as we ensure to give a simplified and the best adventure holiday you deserve.
